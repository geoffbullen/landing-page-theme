Quick start

The order is important! -- Please follow these steps in the order layed out below

In JIRA

  1. Install the WallSync JIRA Add-on (available from the Atlassian Marketplace)
  2. Open one of your Agile Boards, you should see the new WallSync dropdown at the top of the board panel
  3. Select "View/Print Cards". You should get a set of cards generated that inculde the WallSync unique code.
  4. Select "Setup Phone". Confirm your password and email the security token to youself so that you have access to it on your device.

On your mobile or tablet device

  1. Search for the free WallSync mobile app in the app store for your device. Install and Run!
  2. Paste in the token that you emailed to yourself
  3. Select the board that you set up earlier and point your device at the WallSync cards on your screen
