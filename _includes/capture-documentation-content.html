About WallSync Capture

Capture app lets you quickly capture post-it notes on your physical wall and generate issues in your Jira board. Capture is located at Jira navigation panel on the left hand side when you have entered a project page.

Why WallSync Capture

WallSync Capture gives you the following benefits:

  • Quickly sync your physical post-it notes to Jira
  • Tooling to manage issues in Jira

Import your post-it notes

Import your current physical wall of post-it notes into Capture plugin by:

  • Open the phone app
  • Point the camera at the wall
  • Click Capture
  • Confirm the captured columns of cards
  • Click OK to confirm

Update your post-it notes

Your physical wall has changed since the previous workshop, update Capture plugin by:

  • Open the phone app
  • Point the camera at the wall
  • Click Capture
  • Confirm the captured columns of cards
  • Click OK to confirm
