Physical agile story walls are better - number 1 they can actually be more accurate

It sounds counter-intuitive, but physical agile cards can give you more accuracy. This is because of the behaviours they encourage.

A common formula for a standup - “Yesterday I worked on X, today I am working on Y; and I have no impediments”.

It’s simple and it works great, it keeps the everyone up to date with what the team is doing, helps highlight dependencies and gives the scrum master and management the information they need to handle things that prevent the team from being productive. It also helps keep stand ups short (and sweet?).
